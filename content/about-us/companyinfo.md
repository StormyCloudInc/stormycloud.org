      text: |
            StormyCloud Inc is a [501(c)(3)](https://www.guidestar.org/profile/87-3282673 "StormyCloud GuideStar") non-profit organization based in Texas that is dedicated to providing privacy-focused tools and services to allow everyone access to an unfiltered and unregulated internet.
            
            Formed in 2021 to address government restrictions on internet freedom and user privacy, StormyCloud operates various [I2P Services](../i2p-services "I2P Services") to provide secure and private communication channels for users on the I2P network. Learn more about [our mission](../mission "Mission") and see how you can [support our cause](../donate "Donate").
