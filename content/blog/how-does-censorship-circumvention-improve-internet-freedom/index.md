## Methods And Tools For Conquering Censorship

Several resources exist to help people overcome censorship and increase their digital freedom:

**Secure online connections, also known as VPNs:** [Virtual private networks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_private_network) (VPNs) encrypt data transmission, conceal users’ IP addresses, and bypass geo-blocks, allowing them to anonymously and securely access geo-restricted content.

**Businesses that act as intermediaries:** They mediate communication between users and the internet, redirecting inquiries and evading security protocols. By making it appear as though they are connecting from multiple locations, proxies allow users to access prohibited content and websites.

**The Onion Router, or [Tor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tor_(network))**: A privacy-focused browser, Tor, hides its users’ whereabouts and activities from traffic analysis and surveillance with the help of a volunteer overlay network. It enables anonymous browsing and access to blocked content, utilizing circumvention web links to help users navigate around restrictions.

**Mirror Sites:** These act as proxies to prohibited websites, allowing users to access restricted content.

Staying informed about the most recent methods to bypass censorship is crucial for adapting to new circumstances and protecting internet freedom.
