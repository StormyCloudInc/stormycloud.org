Infrastructure, Not Promises

StormyCloud operates real infrastructure — DNS resolvers, cryptocurrency nodes, network services, and more. We've been doing this since 2021, and we're good at it. Now we're extending that capability to other projects that share our values.

If you're building something that advances privacy, open access, or digital rights, we can provide the infrastructure to help you run it. No grants to apply for, no strings attached, no equity conversations. Just servers, bandwidth, and people who know how to keep things running.

We're a self-funded 501(c)(3) nonprofit. We do this because it matters.

What We Provide

Networking

Bandwidth, connectivity, and network infrastructure. We operate our own IP space and can provide dedicated capacity for projects that need reliable, uncensored connectivity.

Compute

Servers, virtual machines, and processing power. From lightweight services to resource-intensive workloads, we can allocate what your project needs to run.

Storage

Persistent data storage and backups. We maintain redundant storage infrastructure and can provide space for repositories, archives, databases, and distributed systems.

Infrastructure

Hosting, deployment, and monitoring. We can run your services alongside ours — same uptime standards, same operational discipline, same infrastructure we trust with our own projects.

Consulting

Technical advisory and architecture guidance. If you're building privacy infrastructure and need experienced eyes on your design, we can help you think through it.
